Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously scrambled egg.

Igredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs
  • 2tbs crem

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a frying oan over a medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs from the edge of the pen to the center every 20 seconds ( as if you are making an omlette)
  4. Whe the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate unite served)
