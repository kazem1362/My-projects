Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are of my favorite food. Here is for deliciuosly rich scrabled.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the egs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Usuing a spatula fold the eggs from the of the pan to the center every 20 seconds( as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on on the plate until served)
